About Me

I am a priest of the Greek Orthodox Archdiocese of America, serving since 2020 at St. Nicholas Greek Orthodox Church in Grand Junction, CO. My ministerial interests include Byzantine chant, liturgics and teaching. I teach an ongoing Byzantine Chant class for the chanters at our parish. I have a long-running personal interest in computer science, and am interested in exploring the applications of computer programming in the realm of liturgics. My recreational interests include hiking, mountain climbing and rock climbing.
